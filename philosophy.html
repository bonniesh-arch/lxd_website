Welcome, Agent!

You're already a creative thinker, and your thinking is the thing AI can't replicate. What AI produces reflects averages, which is a useful starting point, but it doesn't know your audience, context or background the way you do. Your lived experience and original ideas are valuable in the workplace and beyond. The creative agency activities will sharpen how you make decisions about AI: when to bring it in, when to leave it out, and how to stay the author of your own work no matter what. You don't need to be a tech expert to sharpen your creative agency, you just need to be curious.

To get started, we will ask you some questions to help you reflect on what creativity means to you. You are not being tested and there are no correct answers. Rather, you are beginning to explore your creative agency.
