Your thinking is the thing AI can't replicate. What AI produces reflects averages, which is useful as a starting point, but it doesn't know your customer, your brand, or your context the way you do. Your lived experience and original ideas are valuable in the workplace and beyond. Consumers feel the difference between something crafted with intention and something generated without it, even when they can't articulate why. The professionals who thrive with AI aren't the ones who use it most. They're the ones who know how to use it, and when not to.
